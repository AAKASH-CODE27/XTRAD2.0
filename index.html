About XTARD

XTARD 2.0 is an initiative driven by Sairam Institutions to bridge the gap between technology and rural development. Through this hackathon, we actively engage with rural communities to understand their real-world challenges.

As engineers, we are committed to designing innovative, practical, and scalable technological solutions that create meaningful impact. XTARD empowers students to apply their knowledge beyond classrooms, fostering a spirit of innovation, social responsibility, and sustainable development.

Problem Statements

Choose your challenge. Build your solution.

Problem Statement 01

Digital Technology & Governance

Develop digital platforms that strengthen village-level microeconomies by creating interconnected value chains. Solutions should enable a circular rural economy where waste or by-products from one activity become inputs for another. The goal is to foster local entrepreneurship, ensure transparent governance, optimize resource utilization, and generate sustainable employment within rural communities.

Problem Statement 02

Social Inclusion & Empowerment

Design inclusive technological systems that empower biologically, mentally, and physically challenged individuals to actively participate in society. Focus on improving accessibility to education, employment, mobility, communication, and social engagement, enabling differently abled and underprivileged communities to contribute meaningfully to economic and social development.

Problem Statement 03

Energy & Environment

Create innovative and sustainable solutions addressing climate resilience and future energy demands. This includes renewable energy systems, energy-efficient infrastructure, waste-to-energy innovations, climate-adaptive technologies, and sustainable cooling or heat management systems for modern data and AI-driven environments.

Problem Statement 04

Agri-Tech (Rural Innovation)

Develop technology-driven solutions that transform agriculture and rural livelihoods. Focus on improving farm productivity, supply chain efficiency, soil and water management, crop monitoring, and market access while ensuring inclusivity for smallholder farmers and rural communities.

Problem Statement 05

Healthcare

Build practical, scalable healthcare solutions addressing real-world challenges across rural, semi-urban, and urban regions. Solutions should focus on improving accessibility, efficiency, early diagnosis, and delivery of healthcare services to underserved populations.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this Hackathon an online event or offline?

+

This is an offline event.

Is there any registration fee?

+

Registration fee is only for shortlisted teams

Should you stay on campus overnight?

+

Yes, since it's a 24-hour hackathon, we highly encourage you to stay on campus to build without interruptions.

What are the facilities arranged by the organizers?

+

We provide food, snacks, high-speed Wi-Fi, working spaces, and resting areas. Mentors will also be available to help you throughout the event.

I am a newbie, can I hack?

+

Absolutely! Hackathons are a great place to learn. We will have beginner-friendly problem statements and mentors to guide you.

Does this hackathon cover both software and hardware projects?

+

Yes, we welcome both software and hardware innovations that solve rural challenges.

What is the allowed team size for the hackathon?

+

Your team can consist of 3 to 4 members. A mix of skills (design, coding, presentation) is highly recommended.

What should I bring for the hackathon?

+

Bring your laptop, chargers, water bottle, any hardware components you need, and a lot of enthusiasm!

Will you sponsor our travel costs?

+

Travel costs are typically self-sponsored, but local transportation from specific points may be provided. Check our updates for more details.

Will there be any mentoring sessions?

+

Yes, industry experts and experienced professors will be circulating to evaluate your progress and provide valuable feedback.

Is inter-college participation allowed?

+

Yes, inter-college teams are welcome to participate and compete!

Can I change my team members after registration?

+

No, you cannot change your team members after registration.
